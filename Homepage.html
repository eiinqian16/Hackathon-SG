About VOLMATCH


Volmatch is a platform that connects passionate individuals with meaningful volunteer opportunities in their communities by matching them with their personalities.

Our platform connects individuals with volunteer opportunities by matching them with their MBTI personalities.

Our mission:

Empower individuals to find their passion for volunteering.

Connect volunteers with organizations that need their skills and experience.

Promote a culture of service and giving back to the community.
